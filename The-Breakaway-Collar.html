The Breakaway Collar™

By providing instant, easy access to the flange area of a fire hydrant, the Breakaway Collar™ allows a displaced hydrant to be remounted or replaced within minutes.

With no need to break the concrete, fire protection is swiftly restored and the costs associated with fire hydrant repair are dramatically reduced.

Key Features

Security Bolts

Patented bit shape keeps the Breakaway Collar™ tamperproof and secure.

Slip Resistant Surface

Allows pedestrian traffic to move safely.

Rigid Exterior

A rigid exterior with rebar bolts binds the Breakaway Collar™ to the surrounding concrete.

Modular Design

With zero moving parts, the Breakaway Collar™ is easy to install.
