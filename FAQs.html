FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Breakaway Collar™ compatible with all fire hydrants?

The Breakaway Collar™ is designed to be used with all wet barrel fire hydrants from major manufacturers, including Clow, Jones, and Mueller.

Are you suggesting that we retrofit all current fire hydrants with the Breakaway Collar™?

Absolutely not! We recommend the Breakaway Collar™ be installed on new hydrants as well as current hydrants as they’re repaired.

Can the Breakaway Collar™ be customized with our city logo or name?

Sure! Just provide the artwork and we’ll do the rest.

What material is the Breakaway Collar™ made out of?

Using High Density Polyethylene (HDPE) as the main ingredient, we’ve been able to produce a premium product that’s both lightweight and tough enough to last a lifetime.
