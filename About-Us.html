About Us

Hydrant Solutions

Based in Los Angeles, CA, Hydrant Solutions was founded on the idea that fire hydrant maintenance and repair should be quick, safe, and cost-efficient.With over 20 years of industry experience, the founders set out to design the Breakaway Collar out of a need for a less expensive and time-consuming solution for fire hydrant repair.

Since then, the Breakaway Collar has become the most cost-effective, long-term solution for hydrant management in America, helping cities keep costs down while eliminating disruptive repairs that interrupt fire protection for both commercial and residential areas.Innovative, low cost, and 100% made in the USA, the Breakaway Collar is the best way to install a fire hydrant.
